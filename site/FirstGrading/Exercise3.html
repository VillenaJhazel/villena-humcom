How to make Rice Wine "Tapey"

  1. Prepare these Materials & Ingreidents

    • Pot
    • Fress banana leaves
    • Elastic rope
    • Rice
    • "Bubud"
      • It is a small dried white circle that contains fermented rice mixed with cassava
    • Water

  2. Put the rice (any kind of ordinary rice) in the pot (must have a cover).

  3. Add "bubud" to the rice in the pot with a ratio of 2 kilos rice=1 "bubud".

    • Don't crack the "bubud" or powderize because it will not make the rice wine taste good.

  4. Cover the pot with fresh banana leaves.

  5. Tie the leaves using the elastic rope around the pot's neck.

    • Make sure air does not come into the pot.

  6. Cover the pot with it's cover and store in a dry place.

  7. Wait for a month for the rice to be fermented.

    • If you want to have a stronger wine to make you drunk, just store it for 2-3 days.

  8. After that you will open the pot and your "Tapey" is ready to drink.

    • Usually, others add sugar to it if you want the wine to be more sweet.

Have Fun!
